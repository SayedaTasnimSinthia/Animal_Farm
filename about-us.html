About Us

Animal Farm is an online platform dedicated to providing animals and animal-based products with a focus on quality, care & responsibility. We aim to create a trusted space where customers can access reliable information & make informed choices.

We work with verified and responsible suppliers who follow appropriate standards for animal welfare & ethical sourcing. Each animal is handled with care, and health and safety checks are prioritized before availability.

In addition to live animals, we also offer a variety of animal-related products designed to meet practical needs. Our catalog is regularly updated to ensure accuracy, transparency, and quality.

Our goal is to provide a smooth & dependable experience for customers while maintaining strong standards of care, safety & compliance throughout every stage of the process.

We are committed to clear communication, responsible practices & ongoing support for our customers.
