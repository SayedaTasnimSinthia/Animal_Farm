FAQ

1. What is Animal Farm 360 & what do you sell?

Animal Farm is an online store that offers animals & animal-based products. This includes healthy, ethically sourced animals & related goods, depending on availability & legal regulations.

2. Do you sell live animals or only animal products?

We may offer both live animals and animal-based products depending on regional laws, ethical guidelines & stock availability. Availability is clearly listed on each product page.

3. Are the animals healthy & ethically sourced?

Yes. All animals are sourced from verified, licensed breeders or suppliers & undergo basic health checks before sale. We prioritize animal welfare standards & legal compliance.

4. How is delivery handled for live animals?

Live animals are transported using specialized, regulated methods to ensure safety & minimal stress. Delivery times vary by location & customers must be available to receive the animal upon arrival.

5. What are your working hours and how can I contact support?

Working hours are Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (EST). Customer support is available during these hours via email or contact form, with responses typically within one business day.
